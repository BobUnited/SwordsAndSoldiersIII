Instructions:
The object of the game is to destroy your opponent's castle. Do this by spending gold on troops and mana on spells.
Workers produce gold. Each worker costs 100 more gold then the worker before it, to a maximum of 6 workers.
Capture one of the floating swords by sending a troop to walk through it, and mana/gold production will increase.
Hit "Play" to play vs. someone online, or "Play AI" to instantly start playing against an AI.
